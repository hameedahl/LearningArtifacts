The Two Steps to Central Dogma

Genome => Proteins

Step 1 Transcription: DNA (unwounded) is transcribed into useful RNA

  • mRNA = one gene
  • mRNA (messenger RNA) ~ single stranded & able to leave the nucleus
  • A→U, T→A, G→C, C→G

Step 2 Translation: going from a nucleic acid to a protein

  • Requirements: Ribosomal RNA + tRNA
  • Ribosomal RNA: stabilization and bonding site
  • tRNA (transfer RNA): brings the amino acid that corresponds to the RNA sequence
  • the RNA copy is translated into a functional protein
  • Ribosome reads the codons on the mRNA and finds matching tRNA
  • matching tRNA = matching amino acids
  • A→U, U→A, G→C, C→G
